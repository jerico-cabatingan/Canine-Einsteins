Meet Our team

Macy

Headmaster

"I just love dogs so much I decided to style my hair so it matches my poodle. I work with great people and dog-lovers alike. I'm passionate about education too which I guess makes me super qualified."

Heidi

Doggy PE teacher

"I believe the physical health of our students is one of the most important factors in them succeeding in our program. Therefore I make sure every dog under my watch gets 30 minutes of play-time between lessons."

Nikko

Telepath

"I was abandonned by my parents when I was young and raised by wolves, learning to talk to dogs non-verbally and being a mute. I only became literate when I was 14 and struggled to find work. But Canine Einsteins gave me the opportunity of a lifetime. Now I help to teach dogs some pretty high level human concepts."

Barktholomew

Certified Good Boy

"woof woof ruf wof woff.."

Translated- "I graduated from canine einsteins with a masters in obedience and fetch. I love long walks.... long ones. And I work full time as a human-canine liason officer for this fine institution."
